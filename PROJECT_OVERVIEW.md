# Project Overview: BusinessPilot AI

BusinessPilot AI is an autonomous, multi-agent operations platform designed to transform raw business lead lists into structured, prioritized pipelines.

---

## 1. Vision Statement
To bridge the gap between complex AI agents and day-to-day business operations by providing a transparent, modular, and observer-friendly platform where AI is an explainable coworker rather than a black box.

---

## 2. The Problem
B2B sales teams lose hundreds of hours to operational overhead:
- **Inefficient Ingestion**: Lead lists are compiled manually in spreadsheets.
- **Unclear Prioritization**: Sales teams score leads based on gut feeling or obscure rules.
- **Disconnected Systems**: Scoring data, analytics, tasks creation, and reporting are handled in isolated software.
- **Black-Box AI**: Sales reps don't trust automated scoring because they don't know *why* the AI prioritized a lead.

---

## 3. The Solution (BusinessPilot AI)
Our platform orchestrates a team of specialized AI agents to automate the sales workflow in a single click:
1. **Ingests & Normalizes Data**: Ingests custom lead lists with automatic fallback to sample data.
2. **Prioritizes transparently**: Scores leads using a 100-point multi-factor rule and exposes point-by-point justifications.
3. **Generates Insights**: Summarizes sales trends, industry values, and market advisory notes.
4. **Distribute Actions**: Translates executive summaries into actionable Kanban tasks.
5. **Compiles Reports**: Generates HTML, Markdown, and PDF executive reports.
6. **Dispatches Notifications**: Emails stakeholders with operations details.

---

## 4. Key Capabilities

- **Model Context Protocol (MCP)**: Decouples database, charting, and emailing utilities into distinct servers, providing modularity.
- **Explainable AI (XAI)**: Displays scorecards detailing point additions for every lead.
- **Real-Time Observability**: Renders a live timeline logging agent stages and tool latency.
- **Interactive Task Management**: A Kanban board with buttons to drag tasks across "To Do", "In Progress", and "Completed".
- **Offline Session State**: Allows users to save and reload runs to browse previous metrics without rerunning agents.
